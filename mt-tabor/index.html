The Friends of Mt. Tabor Park are volunteer stewards of Mt. Tabor Park, a picturesque, 197-acre volcanic oasis located in the heart of Southeast Portland, Oregon.

Come explore the park's history and see how you can help support our efforts to keep Mt. Tabor safe, clean, and beautiful.

Support the Park

The Friends of Mt. Tabor Park relies on the generous support of our volunteers and membership dues to keep the park safe, clean, and accessible to all. Check out our many volunteer opportunites, and please consider becoming a member. Even the smallest contribution of funds or time makes a huge difference!
